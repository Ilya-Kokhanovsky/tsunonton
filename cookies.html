Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

1. Essential Operations

Tsun On TON ($TSUN) uses minimal essential browser storage (cookies and Web Storage like localStorage) to ensure functionality and improve user experience across the website and Telegram game.

2. What We Store

We strictly adhere to a no-tracking policy. We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or marketing cookies.

  • Theme Preference: The main site stores a theme value (e.g., "dark" or "light") to persist your preferred visual theme across pages.
  • Authentication & Validation: We may use localized storage combined with Telegram initialization data to securely process actions, wallet linkages, and game updates securely without maintaining server-side user mapping.
  • Cookie Consent: A simple acknowledgement indicating you have read the cookie banner.

3. Disabling Functionality

Because our stored properties are essential for core visual functions and decentralized connectivity, attempting to disable or block them may result in unpredictable and deteriorated functionality.

However, you may choose to control or clear localStorage and cookies manually via your web browser settings at any point:

  • Chrome Settings
  • Safari Settings
  • Firefox Settings
  • Edge Settings

4. Consent

By continuing to use the Tsun On TON application or website, you confirm your acceptance of our reliance on strictly necessary storage properties as described in this policy.
