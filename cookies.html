Quick Summary

We only store the minimum needed for the Tsunonton website and Telegram mini-app to work: theme preference, consent, and secure session checks. No ad trackers. No cross-site profiling to our knowledge.

Your Controls

You can clear cookies and localStorage anytime in your browser. Some features may break if you block essential storage.

1. Essential Operations

Tsunonton ($TSUN) uses minimal essential browser storage (cookies and Web Storage like localStorage) to ensure functionality and improve user experience across the website and Telegram GameFi mini-app.

2. What We Store

We aim for a no-tracking policy. We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or marketing cookies on this site.

  • Theme Preference: The main site stores a theme value (e.g., "dark" or "light") to persist your preferred visual theme across pages.
  • Authentication & Validation: We may use localized storage combined with Telegram initialization data to securely process actions, wallet linkages, and game updates without maintaining server-side user mapping.
  • Cookie Consent: A simple acknowledgement indicating you have read the cookie banner.

3. Disabling Functionality

Because our stored properties are essential for core visual functions and decentralized connectivity, attempting to disable or block them may result in unpredictable and deteriorated functionality.

However, you may choose to control or clear localStorage and cookies manually via your web browser settings at any point:

  • Chrome Settings
  • Safari Settings
  • Firefox Settings
  • Edge Settings

4. Consent

By continuing to use the Tsunonton application or website, you acknowledge our use of strictly necessary storage properties as described in this policy. Where required by law, we request consent via the banner.

5. Contact & Transparency

Questions about cookies or storage? Ask in our community chat or review the Knowledge Base. We keep policies public and community-reviewed.
