Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

1. Data Controller & Contact

Tsun On TON ($TSUN) is a decentralized web3 community project operating on the TON blockchain. No legal entity entity acts as a formal data centralized controller. For privacy-related inquiries, you can contact the community team at our official Telegram handle @TsunOnTON.

2. What Personal Data is Processed

To enable functionality and engagement within our GameFi ecosystem, we process the bare minimum data:

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram identifier, parsed when launching our Telegram mini-app, to distinguish accounts and save in-game progress.
  • TON Wallet Address: Your public wallet address, should you choose to connect a Web3 wallet, to facilitate token distributions, potential discretionary rewards, and leaderboards.
  • Theme Preference: A purely localized value stored in your browser's `localStorage` to retain light/dark modes.

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP logs mapped to identities, or physical addresses.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed based on Legitimate Interest to ensure the core game mechanics and Consent implicitly provided by engaging the GameFi mini-app, connecting your TON wallet, or relying on strict essential cookies/localStorage.

4. Users Rights Under GDPR

Subject to technological feasibility (notably regarding immutable blockchains), users residing in the EEA are afforded the right to:

  • Access: Inquire about what data is logged under your Telegram ID.
  • Rectification: Request correction to any localized progress incorrectly attributed.
  • Erasure / Restriction: Disconnect the bot internally to cease processing new actions.
  • Data Portability: Obtain a structured report of your GameFi logs mapped to us.
  • Objection: Refuse future inclusion in aggregated leaderboard exports.

Please contact the team at the address above to exercise these rights.

5. Blockchain Data Immutability

Crucial Notice: Data published directly onto the TON blockchain (including transaction history, token balances, and interacting wallet addresses) is immutable. The Tsun On TON team cannot restrict, alter, or erase data deployed continuously on the global decentralized ledger. By connecting your wallet, you consent to public blockchain accessibility.

6. Data Retention Period

GameFi progression mapping to your Telegram ID is stored as long as you actively participate in the ecosystem. LocalStorage properties last indefinitely until flushed by the user. If you purposefully disconnect the bot, non-blockchain data is set to be naturally superseded. Wallet addresses stored on-chain remain forever.

7. International Transfers

Because we integrate directly with Telegram, structural data logging may be routed through globally distributed Telegram servers outside of the EEA. The project team leverages localized decentralized nodes around the globe. By participating, you accept this decentralized data transfer.

8. Automated Decision-Making & Cookies

Tsun On TON does not subject users to automated profiling for financial or legal decisions. We strictly use localized storage (read our Cookie Policy) and employ no third-party marketing cookies.
