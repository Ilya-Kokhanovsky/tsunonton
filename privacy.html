Tsunonton Privacy Policy

Updated: March 17, 2026

"It's not like we *want* your data anyway, b-baka! We collect the absolute minimum tracking needed to make the game work. Don't get the wrong idea!"

# 1. Data Controller & Your Waifu Guides

Welcome to the Tsunonton Project, an exclusively community-operated decentralized web3 GameFi ecosystem on the TON blockchain. There is no centralized corporate entity tracking you. The Data Controller (if you want to use boring legal terms) is the collective "Tsunonton Project, operated by the community." If you have any privacy concerns, slide into our DMs at our official Telegram @TsunOnTON.

# 2. The Minimal Data We Collect (Really, That's It!)

Unlike those creepy corporate overlords, we don't care about your real name or your dog's birthday. To power our Telegram Mini App & TON GameFi mechanics, we only collect:

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram number for saving in-game progression.
  • TON Wallet Address: Only when you actively connect it, so we can send you rewards and log scores on the blockchain.
  • Theme Preference: A basic string stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage) so you don't go blind opening the app at 3 AM.
Strictly NO IP address logging. NO email collection. NO KYC. NO third-party targeted advertising trackers. We don't sell your data to anyone. End of story.

# 3. Legal Basis (Why We Process Your Data)

Under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA/CPRA, we need a legal excuse to process your Telegram ID. We utilize:

  • Legitimate Interest & Performance of Contract: To run the GameFi app, maintain leaderboards, and execute core gameplay mechanics.
  • Explicit Consent: By opening the Mini App or connecting your Web3 wallet, you consent to this localized and blockchain interaction.

# 4. Blockchain Immutability & Public Nature of Data

Pay Attention: The TON blockchain is a decentralized, immutable public ledger. Your wallet address, token balances, GameFi transactions, and NFTs are publicly visible to everyone forever.

We cannot delete, alter, or restrict anything written to the blockchain. By participating in Web3 and GameFi data protection protocols, you accept full responsibility for your pseudo-anonymity. No Liability for Blockchain Risks: The Tsunonton community is not liable for data published permanently on TON.

# 5. Your Unassailable User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Subject to the absolute limitations of blockchain immutability, you have the right to:

  • Access: Ask us what data we hold (it’s just your Telegram ID).
  • Rectification: Fix incorrect off-chain data.
  • Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten": Tell us to wipe your off-chain Telegram ID mapping from our bot servers.
  • Restriction & Objection: Opt out of our global off-chain leaderboards.
  • Data Portability: Get an export of your GameFi stats.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these, contact @TsunOnTON on Telegram. Include your Telegram User ID. We process requests within 30 days. No fees, no hassle.

# 6. Data Retention Periods & Security

We keep your Telegram ID mapping for a maximum of 3 years after your last login, or until you manually detach your wallet and request deletion. Our security is top-tier: encrypted server communications, renounced token contracts, and zero centralized SQL databases holding personal identifiers.

# 7. International Transfers & Global Nodes

Tsunonton is global. We route data through Telegram's worldwide servers and localized decentralised TON nodes. While we prioritize European nodes when possible, participating means your standard Telegram/Wallet data may traverse jurisdictions outside the EEA. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses implicitly upheld by Telegram infrastructure.

# 8. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Crypto and tsunderes are for adults. Our GameFi ecosystem strictly prohibits users under the age of 13. If you are under 13, close this app immediately. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

# 9. Changes to this Policy

We might update this policy when the lawyers yell at us or new 2026 regulations drop. We provide a 30-day notice via our Telegram Announcement channel before critical changes take effect. Continued use means you accept the updated terms.
